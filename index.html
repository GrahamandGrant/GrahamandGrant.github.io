Design

We spent countless hours working to make the perfect umbrella for any situation. Warmbrella combines comfort, style, and technology in its revolutionary design.

Handle

  • Charging Port
    • In the base of the handle is a USBC charging port which allows for a 1 hour charging time, and the battery can last up to 5 hours of use. You can see how much battery is left through an indicator light at the bottom of the grip. We know that every minute can count in getting from A to B, so we designed the microheater to run at full speed until the last drop of battery.
  • Rubber Grip
    • A textured rubber handle allows for a comfortable grip by any age or size, while also containing the micro heater/fan that powers our micro heatblast technology. Opening the Warmbrella is easy with our built in pressure sensor in the handle- simply squeeze firmly on the handle to open the umbrella.

Canopy

  • Acrylic Material
    • The canopy frame contains micro holes through which bursts of heated air prevent sideways wind and rain, while keeping you warm when traveling.
    • We designed our canopy to mimic the same heating elements used in heated patio umbrellas. So you know how dry and warm you will feel while on the go.
    • The fabric is composed of a water and pressure resistant acrylic, that contains a fire resistant finish.

Frame

  • The fiberglass shaft and ribs make the entire umbrella lightweight, yet sturdy enough to keep from being battered by heavy winds. Heating Element

Tiny holes drilled into the canopy frame allow for the microblast heating technology to keep you warm in any weather. The bursts of heated air keep out any weather that is blown sideways or underneath you, making you feel as secure as being inside.
